Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NerdX

Does NerdX work with ZIMSEC and Cambridge curricula?

Yes! NerdX supports both ZIMSEC and Cambridge curricula. You can practice with questions aligned to either curriculum, making it perfect for students in different educational systems. Switch between curricula anytime to maximize your exam readiness.

How does the credit system work?

Credits are used to access premium features like AI Teacher Mode, Project Assistant, and Virtual Labs. New users get 150 free credits. Credits expire after 1 month from purchase, so use them wisely! Different features cost different amounts of credits based on their complexity.

What subjects are available?

For O-Level: Mathematics, Combined Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), and English. For A-Level: Pure Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, and Biology. All subjects are aligned with both ZIMSEC and Cambridge syllabi.

Is the app available for both iOS and Android?

Yes! NerdX is available for both iOS and Android devices. We're currently awaiting App Store and Google Play Store approval. In the meantime, you can download the app directly from our website.

Can I switch between ZIMSEC and Cambridge curricula?

Absolutely! You can switch between curricula at any time in the app settings. This flexibility allows you to practice with both curriculum styles and maximize your exam preparation.

What happens to unused credits?

Credits expire after 1 month from the date of purchase. Any unused credits will be lost at the end of the month, so make sure to use them regularly for the best learning experience.
